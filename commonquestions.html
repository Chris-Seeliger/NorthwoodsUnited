Common Questions

What equipment is needed?

  • Shin guards, long socks, and a soccer ball of correct size.
  • Cleats are not required but highly recommended.
  • Typically, at the age of 5 and older kids start wearing cleats to provided traction on outdoor surfaces

    • What size soccer ball do we need?

  • Ages 4-8 use size 3 ball (Typically U5 to U10)
  • Ages 8-12 use size 4 ball (Typically U11 to U12)
  • Ages 12 and up use size 5 ball (Typically U13 and up)

    • What is the cost?

  • Rec league cost vary by age with and tend to range from $40.00 to $130.00 per player.
  • Spring/Fall Competitive teams cost vary as well based on the number of tournaments they play and if they par take in the Central Wisconsin Soccer League. Additional fees for travel expenses as well as uniform kits.

    • Do parents need to Volunteer?

  • Yes, the club is a volunteer-based program and volunteer help is needed. We ask parents to help with several tasks. If everyone helps there is less work for all.
  • Being an active board member, a coach or assistant coach, field set up days, field take down, painting of the fields on a weekly to bi-weekly schedule. Additional committees are needed, trash pickup, concession stand persons.

    • Are there bathrooms at the fields?

  • Most games are played at the AVW School grounds and while the school has bathrooms it is not open for youth soccer.
  • The soccer club has portable potties located at the fields.
